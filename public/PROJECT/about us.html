About VNKNOWN

Rooted in our legacy of raising trophies and inspired by our passion for victory.

We Are VNKNOWN

VNKNOWN Entertainment & E-Sports is a global esports joint venture that owns and operates the ultra-successful VNKNOWN teams, along with teams in competitive gaming segments that
include Dota 2, Super Smash Bros., Valorant, Wild Rift, Overwatch Contenders, Splitgate. Building upon the rich legacy of VNKNOWN, it will expand its teams globally,celebrate new victories,
and create even more opportunities for fans to embrace VNKNOWN's gaming culture, content, and players in every corner of the world.

OUR LEADERSHIP:

SULLIVAN MARSH - CEO

Joe Marsh is CEO of VNKNOWN Entertainment & Sports, a global esports joint venture created by Comcast Spectacor in August 2020. He oversees
operations based in Los Angeles and is responsible for the growth and optimization of VNKNOWNNs's global presence and expansion into new
businesses and revenue streams surrounding competitive gaming culture and lifestyle, content, and merchandise.

KATE WILLIAMS - Deputy CEO

Kate previously served as Chief Business Officer of Comcast's Spectacor Gaming division and the Philadelphia Fusion esports franchise that
competes in the Overwatch League.

TONY SHARKS - Founder

Tony was named Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of the Fusion in 2018, providing direct oversight of financials for the team,
including budgeting, forecasting, and long-range planning. Prior to joining Spectacor Gaming and the Philadelphia Fusion, Tony held several finance
positions at Comcast Spectacor, where he was responsible for the development, coordination, consolidation and communication of the company's
annual budgeting, long-range planning and forecasting functions for Comcast Spectacor's core businesses.
